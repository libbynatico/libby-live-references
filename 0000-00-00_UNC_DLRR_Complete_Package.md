**Legal Basis for Review**
--------------------------

The following provisions establish that the Ministry has discretionary
authority and that a G1 restart is not mandated by statute:

**Highway Traffic Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. H.8, section 32(5):**

> *"The Minister may issue a driver's licence to any person who meets
> the requirements of this Act and the regulations authorizing the
> person to drive on a highway."*

The word "may" confers discretionary authority. It does not mandate a
single pathway for all applicants regardless of history.

**Ontario.ca --- Ontario Photo Card page:**

> *"If you want to re-apply for your Ontario driver's licence later, you
> may need to take all the tests, serve mandatory wait times and wait
> for your licence to be processed."*

The phrase "may need to" is conditional and discretionary. It is not
"must" or "shall."

**Ontario.ca --- Reinstate a suspended driver's licence:**

> *"3--10 years invalid: You will have to complete the graduated
> licencing system. You must successfully complete a vision screening
> test, a knowledge test, and the G1 exit and G2 exit road tests to
> reinstate your driving privileges. You are not required to serve the
> mandatory waiting periods between tests."*

This policy confirms that even under the most restrictive
interpretation, the subject can fast-track through all tests without
waiting periods.

**Ontario Regulation 341/94, s. 2(1):**

> *"An applicant for a Class G driver's licence may be issued a new
> licence without taking the applicable examinations \[\...\] if he or
> she held a licence of the same class applied for that was valid at any
> time within the three years before making the application."*

The regulation uses "may be issued" --- permissive, not mandatory. Its
silence about persons outside the 3-year window does not create a
mandatory G1 restart.

**No provision in the Highway Traffic Act, Ontario Regulation 340/94,
Ontario Regulation 341/94, or the Photo Card Act, 2008 mandates a full
G1 restart for a previously fully licensed driver whose licence was
cancelled through photo card issuance.**
