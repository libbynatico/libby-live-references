**SECTION 2 -- Background and Context of Assessment**

Mr. Herbert underwent a psychological assessment intended to determine
his eligibility for adult developmental services.

Although the assessor acted professionally, the assessment did not
incorporate essential elements required under Ontario Regulation 276/10.

Key omissions include:

-- No review of childhood developmental history

-- No examination of school records

-- No integration of lifelong adaptive functioning deficits

-- No developmental framework applied

-- Overemphasis on adult-onset traumatic brain injury

-- Failure to reconcile contradictory findings within the report

As a result, the assessment's conclusions are incomplete and do not
align with the legislative requirements for determining the presence of
a developmental disability.
