**Important: How These Conditions Interact**

*These three conditions do not operate independently. Crohn\'s disease
flares cause systemic inflammation that worsens cognitive fatigue from
TBI. The physical demands of ileostomy management --- which requires
focus, precise motor tasks, and frequent interruption --- are directly
undermined by TBI-related executive dysfunction. Post-surgical recovery
from January 2026 compounds all existing limitations simultaneously. The
combined effect is significantly greater than the sum of the individual
diagnoses. Any assessment of Matthew\'s functional capacity must account
for this interaction, not evaluate each condition in isolation.*
