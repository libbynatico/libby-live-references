I, \[Your Full Name\], am a mentally competent and capable adult under
the Ontario Health Care Consent Act (HCCA). I understand my rights to
informed consent, refusal of treatment, participation in discharge
planning, and the right to request a second medical opinion. I am
exercising those rights as follows:

1\. Refusal of PICC Line Removal

I do not consent to the removal of my PICC line at this time. I require
ongoing Total Parenteral Nutrition (TPN) and am aware that reinsertion
carries significant medical risk and logistical barriers. Removal now
would jeopardize my ongoing care and safety.

2\. Refusal of Hospital Discharge

I do not consent to discharge at this time. My condition remains
unstable for community care, and I have not been provided with:

-   A coordinated home care plan or discussion of community TPN support

-   Follow-up arrangements with my specialist(s) or community physician
    > ("Forbes") despite my repeated requests

-   Medical imaging (such as X-ray) since the removal of my last
    > drainage tube, which prevents confirmation of my concerns about a
    > partial or ongoing obstruction

3\. Medical Concerns Supporting This Refusal

-   My ostomy output remains at unsafe high-output levels

-   My ongoing bowel dysfunction and pain are being downplayed and
    > dismissed

-   No diagnostic reassessment (imaging) has been performed to rule out
    > persistent obstruction

-   Discharge planning appears to disregard my continuing high-risk
    > status and the absence of specialist involvement

4\. Request for Second Opinion

Under the principles of the Ontario Patient Bill of Rights and Health
Care Consent Act, I request:

-   A second opinion from my specialist and/or Dr. Forbes before any
    > decision on discharge

-   A review of my case by another physician not currently involved in
    > my care team

-   Full documentation of my request and this refusal in my medical
    > record

5\. Acknowledgement of Capacity

I confirm that I fully understand the risks of refusing discharge and
PICC removal. I understand that this decision is against medical advice
and request that it be documented as such. I make this decision
voluntarily and without coercion.

I request that this letter be placed in my hospital chart immediately,
and that a copy signed by receiving staff be returned to me for my
records.
