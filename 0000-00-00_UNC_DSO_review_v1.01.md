**2. Legislative and Policy Framework**
---------------------------------------

Under the Services and Supports to Promote the Social Inclusion of
Persons with Developmental Disabilities Act, 2008 (SIPDDA), eligibility
requires:

1.  Significant limitations in cognitive functioning

2.  Significant limitations in adaptive functioning

3.  Evidence that these limitations were present before age 18

DSO's eligibility review process further requires the application entity
to:

-   reassess the evidence

-   ensure that SIPDDA definitions are applied correctly

-   consider whether the psychological assessment requires clarification

-   request additional information where needed

-   provide a written Stage One decision within the applicable timeline

This submission is made in accordance with these requirements.
