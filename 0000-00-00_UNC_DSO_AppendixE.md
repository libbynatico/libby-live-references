APPENDIX E -- Personal Statements & Support Letters
===================================================

E-1 Lived Experience Statement
------------------------------

Mr. Herbert has lifelong cognitive and adaptive impairments that affect
daily functioning. He becomes overwhelmed by complex instructions,
unfamiliar situations, and multi-step tasks. He requires support to
understand, organize, and follow through on essential responsibilities.

E-2 Functional Impact Summary
-----------------------------

Home:

\- Difficulty managing routines

\- Forgetting steps

\- Disorganization

Community:

\- Difficulty navigating systems

\- Trouble understanding processes

\- Vulnerability in unfamiliar settings

Medical:

\- Difficulty recalling instructions

\- Trouble explaining symptoms

\- Needs assistance with follow-up

Employment:

\- Slow learning curve

\- Difficulty multitasking

\- Cognitive fatigue

E-3 Barriers to Accessing Services
----------------------------------

Mr. Herbert struggles to complete forms, follow timelines, understand
requirements, and communicate effectively in bureaucratic settings.
These barriers stem from developmental impairments.

E-4 Navigation Support Requirements
-----------------------------------

He requires support to:

\- Understand instructions

\- Navigate government/medical systems

\- Complete forms

\- Manage appointments

\- Organize information

\- Communicate needs

Without support he becomes overwhelmed and unable to complete tasks.

E-5 Supplemental Statements
---------------------------

Additional context supports that Mr. Herbert's impairments have been
consistent across childhood, adolescence, and adulthood.
