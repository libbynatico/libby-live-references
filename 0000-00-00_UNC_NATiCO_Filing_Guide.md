5\. Purposes of the Corporation

*Copy and paste the following into the "Purposes" field exactly as
written:*

\(a\) To provide information, education, and system-navigation support
to individuals living with disabilities, chronic illness, or complex
medical conditions.

\(b\) To assist individuals in understanding and accessing public
programs, social supports, health services, and community resources.

\(c\) To develop tools, guides, and educational materials that help
individuals and families navigate medical, social, and administrative
systems.

\(d\) To conduct advocacy, research, and public education related to
accessibility, disability rights, and barriers to services.

\(e\) To collaborate with professionals, service providers, and
organizations to improve access to services for persons with
disabilities.

\(f\) To carry on activities that are incidental and conducive to the
attainment of the above purposes.

6\. Restrictions on Activities

Select: No restrictions on activities.

(This is the standard selection for most non-profit corporations.)

7\. Classes of Members

If the form asks about membership classes, enter:

**One class: Voting Members**

Voting Members shall have the right to receive notice of, attend, and
vote at all meetings of members. Voting Members shall be entitled to one
vote per member on each matter submitted to a vote.

8\. Statement of Intent to Solicit

If asked whether the corporation intends to solicit donations from the
public:

**Select: Non-soliciting corporation**

This is the simpler category. It means fewer financial reporting
requirements. You can change this later if the organization begins
accepting public donations.

10\. After Filing --- Immediate Next Steps

Once you receive the Certificate of Incorporation and Corporation
Number:

1.  Hold the first organizational meeting (sign the Board Resolution ---
    see separate document).

2.  Adopt the bylaws (see separate Bylaws document).

3.  Register for a Business Number (BN) with the Canada Revenue Agency.

4.  Open a non-profit bank account (bring Certificate of Incorporation,
    bylaws, and board resolution).

5.  Set up info\@natico.ca email through Google Workspace or
    Microsoft 365.
