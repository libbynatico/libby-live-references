**5. How OT and PT Work Together**

Although OT and PT serve different primary functions, they are
complementary in my situation and are most effective when coordinated:

  ------------------------------------------------------------ -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Physiotherapy focuses on:**                                **Occupational Therapy focuses on:**
  Building the physical capacity to perform movements safely   Adapting the environment and routines so those movements are possible
  Improving strength, mobility, and endurance                  Identifying tools, equipment, and strategies to reduce the physical demand of tasks
  Managing pain and preventing further physical decline        Managing cognitive load, safety, and independence in daily activities
  ------------------------------------------------------------ -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Example: PT works on building the leg strength and stability needed to
transfer from bed safely. OT simultaneously assesses whether a bed rail,
adjusted height, or adaptive technique makes that transfer safer until
that strength is built --- and beyond.
