RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
====================================

NATiCO Disability Services Canada
---------------------------------

**Date:** \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_, 2026

**Location:** Written resolution in lieu of meeting, pursuant to the
Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act.

The undersigned, being all of the directors of NATiCO Disability
Services Canada (the "Corporation"), hereby consent to and adopt the
following resolutions:

1. Adoption of Bylaws
---------------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the bylaws annexed hereto as the initial bylaws of
the Corporation are hereby adopted as the bylaws of the Corporation,
subject to confirmation by the members at the first meeting of members.

2. Admission of Initial Members
-------------------------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the following persons are admitted as Voting Members
of the Corporation:

-   Matthew Herbert

-   Kellsie Hnatejko

3. Appointment of Officers
--------------------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the following persons are appointed to the offices
set out beside their names, to hold office at the pleasure of the Board:

**President:** Matthew Herbert

**Secretary-Treasurer:** Kellsie Hnatejko

4. Fiscal Year
--------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the fiscal year of the Corporation shall end on
December 31 of each year.

5. Banking
----------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the Corporation is authorized to open a bank account
at a Canadian financial institution to be selected by the officers, and
that any two (2) of the following persons are authorized as signing
officers for the purposes of the Corporation's banking:

-   Matthew Herbert, President

-   Kellsie Hnatejko, Secretary-Treasurer

6. Registered Office
--------------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the registered office of the Corporation is
confirmed at:

2B-275 Sheldon Drive

Cambridge, Ontario N1T 1A3

7. CRA Registration
-------------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the officers are authorized to register the
Corporation for a Business Number with the Canada Revenue Agency and to
apply for such program accounts as may be required.

8. General Authority
--------------------

BE IT RESOLVED THAT the officers are authorized to take all actions and
execute all documents necessary to give effect to the foregoing
resolutions.

**The undersigned directors hereby sign this resolution effective as of
the date written above.**
