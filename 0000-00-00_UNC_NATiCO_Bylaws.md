BYLAWS
======

NATiCO Disability Services Canada
---------------------------------

*Enacted by the Board of Directors on
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_, 2026*

Article 1 --- Definitions
-------------------------

**1.1** In these bylaws:

-   "Corporation" means NATiCO Disability Services Canada.

-   "Board" means the Board of Directors of the Corporation.

-   "Director" means a member of the Board.

-   "Member" means a Voting Member of the Corporation.

-   "Act" means the Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act, S.C.
    > 2009, c. 23.

Article 2 --- Registered Office
-------------------------------

**2.1** The registered office of the Corporation shall be located in the
Province of Ontario at such address as the Board may determine by
resolution.

**2.2** The Board may change the address of the registered office within
the province by resolution.

Article 3 --- Members
---------------------

**3.1 Membership Class.** The Corporation shall have one class of
members, designated as Voting Members.

**3.2 Admission.** Voting Members shall be admitted by resolution of the
Board.

**3.3 Rights.** Each Voting Member in good standing is entitled to
receive notice of, attend, and vote at all meetings of members. Each
Voting Member shall have one vote.

**3.4 Termination.** Membership may be terminated by voluntary
resignation in writing, by resolution of the Board for cause, or by
death.

**3.5 No Fees.** No membership fees shall be required unless established
by resolution of the Board.

Article 4 --- Meetings of Members
---------------------------------

**4.1 Annual Meeting.** An annual meeting of members shall be held once
in each fiscal year at a time and place determined by the Board.

**4.2 Special Meetings.** Special meetings of members may be called by
the Board or upon the written request of not less than five percent of
the Voting Members.

**4.3 Notice.** Notice of a meeting of members shall be sent to each
Voting Member not fewer than 10 days and not more than 60 days before
the meeting. Notice may be sent by mail or electronic means.

**4.4 Quorum.** A quorum at any meeting of members shall be a majority
of the Voting Members.

**4.5 Voting.** Questions arising at any meeting of members shall be
decided by a majority of votes. Voting may be conducted by show of
hands, ballot, or electronic means.

**4.6 Electronic Participation.** Members may participate in meetings by
telephone or other electronic means that permit all participants to
communicate adequately. A member participating by such means is deemed
present at the meeting.

Article 5 --- Board of Directors
--------------------------------

**5.1 Number.** The Board shall consist of a minimum of one (1) and a
maximum of five (5) directors.

**5.2 Election.** Directors shall be elected by the Voting Members at
the annual meeting.

**5.3 Term.** Each director shall hold office for a term of two (2)
years or until a successor is elected.

**5.4 Vacancy.** The Board may appoint a person to fill a vacancy on the
Board for the remainder of the vacated term.

**5.5 Removal.** A director may be removed by ordinary resolution of the
members at a special meeting called for that purpose.

**5.6 Powers.** The Board shall manage or supervise the management of
the activities and affairs of the Corporation.

Article 6 --- Meetings of Directors
-----------------------------------

**6.1 Meetings.** The Board shall meet at least once per year.
Additional meetings may be called by any director with reasonable
notice.

**6.2 Notice.** Notice of a board meeting shall be given at least 5 days
before the meeting by mail or electronic means.

**6.3 Quorum.** A quorum for the transaction of business at any board
meeting shall be a majority of directors.

**6.4 Electronic Participation.** Directors may participate in board
meetings by telephone or other electronic means. A director
participating by such means is deemed present.

**6.5 Written Resolutions.** A resolution in writing signed by all
directors entitled to vote on that resolution is as valid as if it had
been passed at a meeting of directors.

Article 7 --- Officers
----------------------

**7.1 Officers.** The Board shall appoint a President, a Secretary, and
a Treasurer. The Board may appoint such other officers as it deems
necessary.

**7.2 Multiple Offices.** One person may hold more than one office.

**7.3 President.** The President shall preside at meetings of the Board
and of the members and shall have general supervision of the affairs of
the Corporation.

**7.4 Secretary.** The Secretary shall maintain records and minutes of
meetings.

**7.5 Treasurer.** The Treasurer shall keep proper accounting records
and present financial statements at the annual meeting.

Article 8 --- Financial Matters
-------------------------------

**8.1 Fiscal Year.** The fiscal year of the Corporation shall end on
December 31 of each year.

**8.2 Banking.** The Board shall designate the banking institution and
authorize signing officers for the Corporation.

**8.3 No Distribution.** No part of the income or property of the
Corporation shall be distributed to its members, directors, or officers,
except as reasonable compensation for services rendered.

Article 9 --- Amendments
------------------------

**9.1** These bylaws may be amended by resolution of the Board, subject
to confirmation by the Voting Members at the next meeting of members.

Article 10 --- Dissolution
--------------------------

**10.1** Upon dissolution of the Corporation, after payment of all debts
and liabilities, the remaining property shall be distributed to one or
more organizations in Canada that carry on purposes similar to those of
the Corporation.

**ENACTED by the Board of Directors on
