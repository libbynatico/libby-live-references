**Subject: Request for Accessibility Accommodation -- Tablet-Based Assistive Device**
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**To: Ontario Works**
---------------------

**I am requesting approval through Ontario Works discretionary benefits for a tablet-based assistive communication and cognitive-support device, along with required accessibility accessories, to accommodate my documented disabilities.**
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Due to persistent post-concussive symptoms following traumatic brain injury, Crohn's disease with significant fatigue and physical limitations, and documented auditory processing and cognitive challenges, I experience difficulty using standard communication and computing methods. During periods of medical instability, I am frequently bed-bound or unable to tolerate prolonged upright posture.**
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**A tablet-based device with a larger, stable display is required to allow me to read, process, and interact with medical and administrative information without the visual strain, cognitive overload, and physical limitations associated with smartphone use or public computer access. Smaller devices are not usable for extended periods due to visual fatigue, limited display size, and increased cognitive load.**
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Participation in video-based communication using a phone is not feasible, as the screen size prevents effective use of visual compensation strategies such as facial cue recognition and lip-reading, which I rely on to supplement auditory processing and manage audio lag. Audio-only communication is insufficient and results in missed or misunderstood information.**
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Due to cognitive impairment and executive functioning limitations, I am unable to efficiently learn or adapt to new operating systems or unfamiliar device interfaces. Continued use within the Apple ecosystem is required to minimize cognitive load, maintain consistent accessibility settings, and ensure reliable use during periods of fatigue, illness, and recovery. Introducing a different platform would create additional cognitive barriers and significantly reduce the effectiveness of the accommodation.**
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**This device is required to support:**
---------------------------------------

-   **Communication with healthcare providers and service agencies**
    ----------------------------------------------------------------

-   **Reading and completing medical forms and instructions**
    ---------------------------------------------------------

-   **Note-taking during appointments and care coordination**
    ---------------------------------------------------------

-   **Reviewing documents while bed-bound or physically limited**
    -------------------------------------------------------------

-   **Digitally signing documents when paper handling is not feasible**
    -------------------------------------------------------------------

**The requested accessories are required components of this accessibility setup:**
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-   **A protective screen cover to reduce glare and protect the device during frequent handling**
    ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-   **An Apple Pencil or equivalent stylus to allow handwritten notes, annotations, and digital signatures when fine motor endurance or typing is limited**
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-   **A durable protective case with integrated keyboard to allow stable use from bed or seated positions and reduce physical strain during extended use**
    ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Assistive Devices Program (ADP) clarification:**
--------------------------------------------------

**This request is not being submitted through the Assistive Devices Program (ADP) because the required accommodation falls outside ADP program scope. ADP does not fund or authorize multipurpose assistive technology used to address combined and fluctuating cognitive, communication, visual-processing, and fatigue-related impairments. There is no applicable ADP device category or authorizing pathway for this request. Accordingly, this accommodation is being appropriately requested through Ontario Works discretionary accessibility and participation supports.**
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Without this accommodation, there is an increased risk of ongoing communication barriers and difficulty maintaining consistent participation in medical and disability-related processes, resulting in gaps in follow-up and service access.**
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**This request is for accessibility accommodation and disability support and is not for general personal electronics use.**
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Please advise if additional documentation from my healthcare team is required.**
