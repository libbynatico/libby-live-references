8\. SYSTEM FAILURE ANALYSIS

AODA --- Accessibility Failure

Law: Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005, Ontario

Source: https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/05a11

Obligation: Provide accessible communication for people with
disabilities

Failure: TBI not accommodated --- verbal-only communication enforced

Consequence: Unable to participate effectively in services

**Potential Breach: YES**

ODSP --- Administrative Delay

Law: Ontario Disability Support Program Act, 1997, Ontario

Source: https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/97o25b

Obligation: Assign caseworker, provide income support and navigation

Failure: No worker assigned as of March 2026

Consequence: No program access, no advocacy

**Potential Breach: POSSIBLE --- unreasonable administrative delay**

MTO / Driver Control --- Duty to Inform

Law: Highway Traffic Act, R.S.O. 1990, c. H.8, Ontario

Source: https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/90h08

Obligation: Clear reinstatement requirements disclosed before payment

Failure: Payment accepted without disclosure of blocking conditions

Consequence: \~\$5,000 lost; license not restored

**Potential Breach: POSSIBLE --- administrative unfairness / failure to
inform**
